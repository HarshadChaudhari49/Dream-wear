A woman stands in front of her wardrobe and thinks I wish I had something that felt like me today soft but put-together. Rich but never trying too hard. Most of the time, that outfit doesn't exist. She settles or go with the trend. 

We started with a simple belief: I want to give life to my wardrobe. 

This is a platform where a woman can describe the outfit she's been dreaming of the feeling, the fabric, the comfort she wants and watch it become real. Real designs made from real ideas worn by real women. Not mass-produced. Not loud. Not according to trend. Just thoughtful pieces that feel classy, comfortable and unmistakably hers across every season and every mood. 

But this is more than a clothing app. It's a quiet community of women who believe their ideas are worth wearing. Every design here began as someone's dream and basically my dream as well that is something no fast-fashion brand can ever copy. 

Watch how it compounds: 

1. She dreams → we make it → she wears it 

2. With her blessing, it appears on the App 

3. Another woman sees it, loves it, buys it _and_ feels inspired to share her _own_ dream 

4. The first woman sees _"30 women loved your idea"_ → feels proud → tells her friends 

5. More dreams in, more looks out → the wall grows itself 

It answers a powerful question for every new visitor _"Is this real? Does this actually work?"_ and the answer is a wall of real women, real dreams, real outfits. 

Our product will create a loop: _Dream Screen → Designed → Featured here → Bought by someone else_ – the whole emotional ecosystem comes full circle on this one card.

## **Module 1 –** 

Our main dashboard module is the brain of the app showcasing all types of ready-to-buy designs, starting with kurti’s and tops which we will launch in certain time span (expanding to dresses and cord sets in future versions). 

Here's what we're planning for this screen – 

1. Offer Banner – A dynamic promotional banner at the top (use Savana app for reference). 

2. Category Cards – Two large cards below the banner for exploring kurti’s and tops, each designed by real customers and available for anyone to purchase. 

3. New Arrivals – A dedicated section featuring our customers' latest designs. We'll show a banner with an outfit, its detailed description (in dialogue box) and a "Shop Now" button. 

4. Trending Now – This section will highlight designs that have either reached a certain number of successful deliveries or gained viral popularity (planned for the next version). 

Two qualifying triggers: 

Delivery-based trending – e.g., crosses 50/100 successful deliveries 

Virality-based trending – spikes in saves/shares/wish-list activity 

5. Product List – A scrollable list of all available items at the bottom, with options to buy or add to cart. 

## **Module 2 – Explore All Products**

First we will deliver it to the person who represent this idea. 

The photo her wearing the finished piece (real and candid beats over-styled here authenticity _is_ the brand). Otherwise, we will use our modal photos. 

Shop this look button because every inspiring card should also be buyable to every one else. 

A love/save action so others can collect what moves them. 

## **Module 3 –** 

Part 1: The Input Screen – Tell Us Your Vision 

This is the emotional entry point. It should feel less like a form and more like a conversation. 

- Open text space – Describe your dream outfit. No detail is too small (free-flowing, not restrictive). 

- Mood/Vibe tags – (Optional else we will integrate in next version) quick-select chips like Soft, Bold, Classic, Edgy, Romantic, Minimal for women who struggle to put feelings into words. 

- Inspiration upload – Let her add a reference photo, Pinterest screenshot or even a colour swatch. 

- Occasion tags – _Everyday, Celebration, Travel, Just for me_ , which helps designers understand context. 

- A gentle prompt line something like _"What feeling do you want this outfit to give you?"_ this pulls emotion, not just specs. 

This screen should feel like journaling, not filling a form. That's what makes women trust it enough to be honest. 

Part 2: The Showcase Cards — Where Vision Becomes Real 

This is the heart of the module proof that dreams get heard and made. 

Each card includes: 

1. Photo of her wearing the finished outfit. 

2. Her original words below the card displayed exactly as she wrote them, maybe in a slightly handwritten-style font for warmth and easier for others to read. 

Why this works emotionally: 

- Other users browsing will see _real stories_ , not just outfits which builds both inspiration and trust. 

- It subtly says _"Your wardrobe isn't generic. It's personal. And we take that seriously."_ 

Use – 

A scrollable feed of all these cards, almost like a Pinterest board built entirely from real customer dreams. 

Save/Heart button so visitors can save outfits/ideas that resonate with their own mood. 

## **Module 4 –** 

A dedicated space (visible to all women who've shared ideas and inspired those to share who don’t). 

When she opens it, she sees every idea she's ever submitted, each as a soft card: 

- A small thumbnail (her reference image, or a gentle placeholder illustration if none) 

- The first line of her dream, in her own words 

- Date shared and current status of it. 

If she hasn't shared anything yet a warm empty message _"Your dreams will live here. Share your first one?"_ with a button straight to the input screen. 

Below it will display the stories of other people’s who shared (for inspiration). 

She taps a dream (card) → it opens into the full story of that one idea. This is where the _visual_ progress lives in stages with visual images captured while making it. 

And the final action button stays locked with a quiet _"unlocks soon"_ building a little anticipation without frustration. Use lines below each viral idea cards like "12 women loved your idea." 

## **Module 5 – Profile/Settings** 

Most apps make this screen a boring "My Account" a list of settings and order history nobody feels anything about. Ours shouldn't. For a brand built on dreams and comfort, this is where it should feel like she lives inside the app. Her closet, her diary, her story all in one warm, personal space (need to brainstorm while designing). 

## **Previously Bought (My Wardrobe) –** 

Everything she's bought, gathered like a real closet. 

- A soft grid of her purchased outfits each one she can tap to revisit 

- "Wear it again" button on each → straight reorder or repurchase in another colour. 

- Under each piece, show her original dream words if it came from her own idea _"This started as your wish for something soft for work"_ closing the loop beautifully. 

- Her photo + name (optional photo some women prefer privacy, so let it be a soft illustrated avatar/posted image if she skips it) – If it’s inspired by her thoughts. 

- Styling notes (small suggestions): _"Pairs beautifully with…"_ turning her closet into a quiet stylist. 

## **Upload Profile –** 

- A small personal line she can write herself like a bio, but gentler _"Lover of soft fabrics and slow mornings_ " this tiny touch makes the space feel hers, not the app's. 

- Her photo + name and remaining details. 

- A quiet stat line not vanity metrics, but meaningful ones _"3 dreams shared · 5 pieces loved (wish-list)"_ framed warmly, never like a leaderboard. 

## **Saved & Wishlist –**
